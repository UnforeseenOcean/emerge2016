Branger_Briz

Story

The LiveWorx 16 conference partnered with interactive agency Branger_Briz to bring their web-based depth portraits experience to Boston. The installation debuted in April 2016 at the eMerge Americas conference in Miami FL and was later installed at BYOB (Bring Your Own Beamer) at Mana Contemporary in Chicago, IL.

The installation uses a Kinect depth sensor to create realtime 3D stylized portraits. Conference attendees dynamically shape the aesthetic of their portraits through their movements which are displayed live in one of the conference's lounge experiences at Xtropolis. A number of agencies have started to deploy depth sensors in interactive projects over the last handful of years but what set's this installation apart is it's integration with the web. “To our knowledge this project is the first of it's kind to stream depth-data driven portraits directly to a web browser.” Each participant's 3D snapshots are instantly uploaded to the interactive online gallery to be re-experienced and shared on social media.
